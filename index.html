About My GOAT, Arsi Hakita Patala!

Hello, my name is Jack Mossberg. I would like to introduce my 'GOAT' (Greatest Of All Time), Arsi Hakita Patala, otherwise known as 'Hakita'. Online, Hakita is known as the mind behind the hit game Ultrakill and its one-of-a-kind soundtrack.

While I have many reasons for nominating Arsi Hakita as my GOAT, my biggest reason is his unwavering resolve in game development and music composition. This is evident in his dedication to creating the best possible product, even under intense pressure to deliver on his dream game, Ultrakill.

The following is more information about my GOAT, Hakita. Enjoy!

Hakita's Accomplishments

These are some of Hakita's well-known accomplishments!

Accomplishments Resources
Created and Developed Ultrakill: As the main developer, Hakita designed ULTRAKILL, a fast-paced, retro-style first-person shooter inspired by classic games like Quake and Doom, combined with modern mechanics from Doom (2016) and character action games like Devil May Cry. The game was released in Early Access on Steam on September 3, 2020, and has been praised for its high skill ceiling, fluid movement, and intense gameplay. Ultrakill Wikipedia Page
Composed Ultrakill Soundtrack: Under the pseudonym Heaven Pierce Her, Hakita composed nearly all of the gameâ€™s soundtrack, which features a diverse range of genres including breakcore, symphonic metal, black metal, ambient, and lo-fi. The music dynamically shifts based on in-game situations, earning acclaim for its skillful use of leitmotifs and atmospheric enhancement. Ultrakill Developers Fandom Page
Led Game Design and Direction: Handled all direction, game design, sound design, story, animation, and most level design for ULTRAKILL (except for specific levels like the Sandbox and 5-S), creating a cohesive vision that blends retro aesthetics with modern gameplay. Ultrakill Developers Fandom Page
Achieved Critical and Commercial Success: Ultrakill has received "Overwhelmingly Positive" reviews on Steam, with over 159,000 user reviews and a 97% positive rating as of June 2025. It has built a strong fanbase for its unique style-scoring system and challenging gameplay. Ultrakill Steam Page
Spearheaded Major Updates: Oversaw the "ULTRA_REVAMP" update on February 24, 2025, which included a near-complete overhaul of ULTRAKILL with new graphics, technical improvements, "Encore" levels with unique themes, and music from King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard. This update addressed codebase issues to prepare for the Fraud layer (Layer 8). Ultrakill Wikipedia Page
Built a Collaborative Team: Recruited and worked with a diverse team of programmers, artists, and quality assurance members from across the globe, including notable contributors like PITR (lead programmer) and Victoria Holland (lead 3D artist), fostering a community-driven development process. Ultrakill Developers Fandom Page
Developed as a Solo Project Initially: Began Ultrakill in 2018 as a solo project while studying game design in Finland, transitioning to a full-time endeavor after its success allowed him to leave school and focus on development. Ultrakill Wikipedia Page
